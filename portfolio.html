About Me

I'm a native New Orleanean and a Cuban American. While studying music at Loyola University, I caught the coding bug and began deeper exploration of the ever-evolving and far-reaching tech universe. After graduation, I worked for several years in software as an application tester and support administrator, among other roles involving custom software development. I've also worked as an illustrator, a graphic designer, a web development tester, a copy writer, a copy editor, and a technical writer. Through my experience, I've learned that I thoroughly enjoy working through puzzles and creating beautiful solutions.
